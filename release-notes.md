## July 2024
{: #subcollection-jul2024}

### 10 July 2024
{: #subcollection-10jul2024}
{: release-note}

Version 1.3.6 of the Watsonx.ai SaaS with Assistant and Governance deployable architecture available

## June 2024

### 21 June 2024
{: #subcollection-21jun2024}
